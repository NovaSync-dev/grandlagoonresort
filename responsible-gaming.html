Responsible Gaming

At Grand Lagoon Resort & Casino, we believe entertainment should be enjoyable, controlled, and positive. We are committed to creating a safe gaming environment.

Our Principles

  • Gaming should be a form of entertainment, not a source of income
  • Only gamble with money you can afford to lose
  • Set time and spending limits before you play
  • Never chase losses
  • Take regular breaks from gaming
  • Don't gamble when upset, stressed, or under the influence

Self-Exclusion

We offer voluntary self-exclusion programs for guests who wish to limit their access to gaming facilities. Arrangements can be made confidentially through our guest services desk.

Limit Setting

Guests are encouraged to set personal limits on time spent and money wagered. Our staff can assist with practical arrangements to support these limits.

Need Support?

If you or someone you know may have a gambling concern, confidential help is available:

  • National Council on Problem Gambling: 1-800-522-4700
  • Gamblers Anonymous: www.gamblersanonymous.org
  • GamCare: www.gamcare.org.uk

Staff Training

All casino team members receive regular training in responsible gaming practices and are equipped to assist guests discreetly and respectfully.

Age Verification

Casino access is restricted to guests aged 18 and over. Valid photo identification is required. We take our responsibility to prevent underage gambling seriously.
